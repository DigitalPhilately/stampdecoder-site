What does it do?

Modern GB Stamp Decoder explains what the barcode on a modern GB stamp means — in plain English.

You’ll see the same details collectors care about first, plus deeper information that is not visible to the eye.

  • Issue type and value
  • Colour where it is meaningful for the issue
  • Print, pack, issue, and barcode generation dates
  • Origin, supplier, and collector notes
  • Checklist references and premium catalogue numbers when entitled

Who it’s for

Modern GB Stamp Decoder gives you confidence in what you’re holding and helps turn a scan into a usable collector record.

How does it work?

Modern GB stamps contain a machine-readable barcode. Modern GB Stamp Decoder interprets this data and presents it in a clear, collector-friendly flow: scan, check Decode, add it to My Collection, and create a label when useful.

Technical note: supports Royal Mail JGB-format barcodes.
