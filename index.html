FAQ

Quick questions, quick answers.

What barcode format does it read?

Royal Mail JGB-format barcodes — the ones printed on every modern GB stamp from the 2022 changeover onward, including KCIII definitives and regional issues.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Scanning and decoding happen on your device. You don't need a connection in the field, and your collection isn't shipped off to a server.

Do you collect any data about me?

No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your scans, your collection, your labels — they live on your device unless you explicitly export them.

Is there a premium tier?

The core scan + decode + collection flow is free. A premium layer adds catalogue references (SG and similar) and bulk-export tools for serious collectors. You'll see exactly what's included before you opt in.

When does it launch?

Currently in private beta on iOS and Android. Sign up below and tell us which device you're on — we'll send a TestFlight or Play invite when there's a slot.

Is this an official Royal Mail product?

No. Modern GB Stamp Decoder is an independent collector tool. Stamp imagery shown on this site is used under licence — we are not affiliated with Royal Mail Group Ltd.
