What does it do?

Stamp Decoder explains what the barcode on a modern GB stamp means — in plain English.

You’ll see the same details collectors care about, plus information that isn’t visible to the eye.

  • Issue type (Definitive, Special, Regional)
  • Print and production dates
  • Value and format
  • Monarch era (QEII / KCIII)
  • Origin and supply details
  • Hidden security information

Who it’s for

Designed for collectors of modern Great Britain stamps.

Whether you collect:

  • Barcoded Machins
  • KCIII definitives
  • Regional issues
  • First Day Covers
  • Used or mint examples

Stamp Decoder gives you confidence in what you’re holding.

How does it work?

Modern GB stamps contain a machine-readable barcode. Stamp Decoder interprets this data and presents it in a clear, collector-friendly way.

Technical note: supports Royal Mail JGB-format barcodes.
