Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-07

Summary

  • No account required
  • No analytics and no tracking
  • Barcode decoding is performed on-device
  • If you email support, we receive your email and message

Information we process

The app processes barcode strings (JGB / DataMatrix) on your device to decode and display the information contained in the barcode.

We do not collect barcode data from your device automatically.

Support emails

If you contact us at support@stampdecoder.app, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to your request and to improve the app.

Third-party services

We do not use third-party analytics in the app at this time.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. If the app stores any data locally on your device (for example, user preferences), removing the app will remove that local data.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@stampdecoder.app.
