Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

Summary

  • No account required
  • No analytics or tracking in the app
  • Optional Google Analytics on this website, used only after you accept analytics cookies
  • Barcode decoding is performed on-device
  • My Collection/checklist data is stored locally on your device (including saved Mint/Used ownership states)
  • If you email support, we receive your email and message

Information we process

The app processes DataMatrix barcode strings on your device to decode and display the information contained in the barcode.

We do not collect barcode data from your device automatically.

If you use collection/checklist features, that data is stored locally on your device as part of normal app use. Label previews are also generated locally on-device.

Technical note: the app supports the DataMatrix barcodes printed on modern Royal Mail stamps.

Website analytics and cookies

StampDecoder.app uses Google Analytics 4 to help us understand website visits and which pages are useful. Google Analytics is optional and does not load until you select Accept analytics in the website banner.

If you accept, Google Analytics may process information about the pages you visit, how you reached the site, and your browser or device. Analytics cookies may be stored on your device. We have disabled Google Signals and advertising personalisation in our site configuration.

Your choice is stored in your browser so we can remember it. You can reject analytics or change your choice at any time using Cookie settings. See Google's Privacy Policy for information about Google's processing.

Support emails

If you contact us at beta@stampdecoder.app, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to your request and to improve the app.

Third-party services

We do not use third-party analytics in the app. This website uses Google Analytics only after you consent, as described above.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. If the app stores any data locally on your device (for example, user preferences), removing the app will remove that local data.

For the website, you can reject analytics when first asked or revisit Cookie settings to change your choice.
